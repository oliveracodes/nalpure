What Do We Offer?

We are glad you made the decision to visit Iceland! Sometimes choosing between all the different accommodation, tours and offers is not an easy job — especially if it is your first time around. Please don't hesitate to contact us if you should have any questions, special requests or wishes.

At nalpure, we specialize in small group or private adventure tours for every kind of customer. Whether you are planning your own self-drive holiday around Iceland, bringing your best friend on a horseback riding tour, ​experience off the beaten track by foot, or just want to have a fun weekend trip in Reykjavík, you can book everything through us.
